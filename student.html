Frequently Asked Questions


    1. What is the Computer Science Capstone Program?

    The Computer Science Capstone Program is open to all students majoring in Computer Science. It is required that all Computer Science & Business and Computer Science & Engineering students to complete their capstone before graduating.

    2. When do I take my Capstone?

    The Capstone Program is completed over two semesters, starting in the Spring of your junior year and concluding in the Fall of your senior year.

    3. What prerequisites must be completed before Capstone?

    CSE 216 must be completed before registering for Capstone. For CSB students, CSE 241 must also be completed.

    4. What type of projects are offered in Capstone?

    Click here to view past projects.

    5. How do I pick my Capstone project?

    All projects are presented during class, and you will select your preferences within the first week. You'll complete a Google form listing your favorite projects, and a Capstone group and project will be assigned based on your preferences.
