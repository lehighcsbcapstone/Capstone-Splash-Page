High-Impact Projects in an educational environment

Lehigh University's Computer Science and Business (CSB) Capstone program focuses on real-world applications of both business and technology. We see projects through from planning to execution, with an emphasis on project management and teamwork. Students who have participated in the CSB Capstone program find that their experiences are invaluable once they have entered the workplace.
