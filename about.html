About

The CSB Capstone Program is the culmination of the students' coursework at Lehigh, where they apply their skills working on high-impact projects for corporate sponsors.

Sponsors have the opportunity to work with some of the best and brightest students Lehigh has to offer, while simultaneously contributing to their educations.

Faculty

George Witmer

Professor of Practice, Computer Science and Engineering

Computer Science and Business Co-Director

George Witmer earned a doctorate in Chemical Engineering at the University of Houston, an MBA at Lehigh University, and a bachelor's in Chemical Engineering from Brigham Young University. Before joining Lehigh, Witmer worked at Air Products, where he led IT Innovation, and prior to that, R&D Computing. He also worked at PPL and at Lutron as an Enterprise Architect. He has several patents and has presented his work at numerous technical conferences.
