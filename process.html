August

  • Meet with CSB Director of Capstone Projects.
  • Establish scope of project and designated company participants.
  • Begin developing project description.

September

  • Verbal acceptance of CSB Lehigh University Sponsor Agreement key terms.
  • Draft of project description. Be sure you have approval from your legal department.
  • Meet with CSB Director of Capstone Projects to process paperwork.

October

  • Identify project mentors and sponsorship/legal issues.
  • CSB Director of Capstone Projects can meet with legal or executive representatives.

November

  • Sponsor Agreements and Project Descriptions due.
  • Save the date for the January Sponsor Fair!

December

  • Finalize Project Descriptions, identification of project mentors, outstanding paperwork and fees.

January

Semester Begins!
